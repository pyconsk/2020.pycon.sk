PyCon SK is organized by the Python community and for the Python community. All of our staff members are volunteers. We are looking for new members to join the team of organizers. This is a great opportunity to meet people, make new friends and learn new things.

As a volunteer you get a free ticket to the conference as a thank you for making a change in our society. If you are a Slovak citizen and you work at least 40 hours a year as a volunteer, you can donate 3% of your tax liability to any registered non-profit.
